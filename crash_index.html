Upper Manhattan's Pedestrian Safety Crisis

2025 was New York City's safest year on record — and pedestrians still made up over half the traffic deaths. In Harlem, Washington Heights, and Inwood, the danger is designed in: wide arterials, no protected left turns, no daylighting at crosswalks. These neighborhoods have waited longest for the fixes that are already known to work.
